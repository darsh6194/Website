Flutter Application

NAMMA Area Social Media Application For Environmental Concerns

NAMMA Area is an innovative social media platform designed to empower communities to combat environmental pollution through collective action. The platform enables users to capture and upload photos of polluted surroundings, tag relevant government authorities, and share these issues within their communities. By facilitating the formation of volunteer groups, NAMMA Area encourages citizens to organize clean-up drives and collaborate on resolving environmental challenges. The platform also allows users to track the impact of their efforts, fostering a sense of civic responsibility and promoting government accountability. NAMMA Area aims to create a cleaner, healthier environment, enhancing the quality of life for everyone involved.

The NAMMA Area project is built using a modern tech stack designed for efficiency and scalability. The frontend is developed using Flutter, ensuring a responsive and intuitive user experience across multiple platforms, with Dart as the primary programming language. The backend is powered by Node.js with Express.js, providing a robust and scalable server-side environment. Data storage and management are handled by MongoDB, a NoSQL database that efficiently stores user data, images, and reports. This tech stack enables NAMMA Area to deliver a seamless and impactful platform for community-driven environmental action.

Speech Emotion Detection

Speech Emotion Detection Using LSTM

This project focuses on detecting human emotions from speech using a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural network. By analyzing audio signals, the model identifies emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, and more. Leveraging the power of LSTM networks, which are well-suited for sequential data, the project accurately captures the temporal patterns in speech that are indicative of different emotional states. The model is trained on the TESS dataset, which provides a diverse range of speech samples labeled with corresponding emotions. This Speech Emotion Detection system has applications in areas such as customer service, healthcare, and human-computer interaction, where understanding emotions can enhance user experiences and outcomes.
